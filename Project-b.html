Satyug Darshan Vidyalya

The establishment of Satyug Darshan Vidyalaya is a step towards awakening humanity to the supreme truth and to understand the true worth of knowledge. A key objective of initiating this school is to impart such moral values, so that all the actions and the deeds of the supreme creation of God, 'the man,' remains truthfully supreme for establishing an environment of peace and harmony in the Universe. The school aims at providing quality education, convey the message of sympathy, understanding and universal brotherhood to each one of its students.
